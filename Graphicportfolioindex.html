A Critical Analysis of Disney’s “Pocahontas”

For my English 229 class, I conducted a critical analysis of Disney’s “Pocahontas” using both a new historicism and narrative criticism lens. This was my first experience with graphic design as I chose to display my information via an infographic (which would usually be one cohesive, vertical document.) This inforgraphic was made from scratch using the application Canva I quickly realized how much I enjoyed making visual graphics and soon after this project I applied for James Madison's SMAD departement.
