About Me

I am working to attain a double degree from James Madison University in both Creative Writing and Social Media and Design with a concentration in interactive design (this portfolio was made as a final project for one of my interactive design courses). I am passionate about finding new and creative ways to innovate the various mediums that determine how we communicate to one another.

During the past summer, I worked as a content development and marketing intern with Harrisonburg Downtown Renaissance. My job entailed researching and writing regular blog posts, developing video content for HDR’s various social media channels (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube channel, website, etc.), as well as taking ownership of their online directory and events listings, working on their website, email newsletter, social media, community events, and developing print materials.

Currently, I am a senior editor for James Madison’s new literary magazine, “Iris”. James Madison’s literary magazine was dangerously close to losing funding as a result of the pandemic, so a team of passionate English majors, myself included, came together to revitalize the magazine and ensure that JMU students have an outlet to share their creative work.
